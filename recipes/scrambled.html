Egg, Scrambled

A classic. An almost omelette. A mixture, a concoction, a question, a solution

Ingredients

  • Eggs: 2
  • Onion: Red
  • Tomato: Roma
  • Ginger: Some
  • Spices: Indian
  • Salt: Salt

Steps

  1. Nonstick pan + low heat + oil
  2. Chop onion in half. Finely chop half, store other half
  3. Onion in pan, grate ginger(some)
  4. While onion caramelizing, stir occasionally, chop tomato in half.
  5. After onion caramelize, put in tomato(half), store other half
  6. Pinch of salt + stir + cover
  7. Prep roti if making
  8. Open + spice: turmeric, red, coriander, garam, salt(rest)
  9. Crack eggs and mix continously
  10. Gas off when solidies
  11. Serve + clean
