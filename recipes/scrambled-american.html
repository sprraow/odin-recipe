Scrambled(Unseasoned)

Egg, Scrambled, American

Almost as good. Still good.

Ingredients

  • Eggs: 2
  • Cheese: Any
  • Butter: Salted
  • Bread: Sourdough

Steps

  1. Crack eggs in bowl and whisk till u think its whisked
  2. Nonstick pan + lots of butter + low heat
  3. Pour eggs into pan, keep stirring
  4. Cut cheese in small pieces + mix when egg a bit cooked
  5. Salt, dont stop stirring till bit solid
  6. Sourdough toast + butter + egg on top
